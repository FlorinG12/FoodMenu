Crispy Chicken

Crispy Crunch, Flavor Punch

Crispy Chicken

Ingredients

  • 100g Flour
  • 250g chicken breasts , boneless, skinless
  • 4 eggs
  • 300g cornflakes

Instructions

Cut the chicken breasts into four tenders each and soak them in a bowl with the buttermilk and hot sauce for 30 minutes. Add the eggs to one bowl and the flour and spices to a second bowl. Dip each piece of chicken from the buttermilk bowl to the flour mixture. Dip it into the eggs then back into the flour mixture. Shake excess flour gently off and put the chicken onto a baking sheet. Repeat with all the pieces. Heat the oil (three inches deep) in a dutch oven on medium high heat to 350 degrees. Fry the chicken in small batches for 5-7 minutes or until golden brown.

Burger

Savor the Moment, Bite by Bite

Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 Ls olive oil
  • 1 onion, peeled and finely chopped
  • 500g Beef
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 4 slices mature Cheddar
  • 4 white rolls
  • 1 beef tomato, sliced
  • ketchup/any sauce you prefer

Instructions

Heat the olive oil in a frying pan, add the onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened and starting to turn golden In a bowl, combine the beef mince with the herbs and the egg. Season, add the onions and mix well. Using your hands, shape into 4 patties. Meanwhile, lightly toast the cut-sides of the buns on the barbecue. Fill with the lettuce, burgers and tomato slices. Serve with ketchup, if you like.

Pizza

Pizza

Ingredients

  • 500g Flour
  • 350ml Hot Water
  • 25g Yeast
  • 2 Ls Sugar
  • 2 Ls Salt
  • 2 Bs Olive Oil

Instructions

First dissolve yeast in warm water , and also add the sugar , salt and oil in that bown with water. Second put the flour in a bigger bowl and add the water mix on top, then start kneading for 20-30mins. If the pizza dough is elastic, then it is ready to put in the fridge for 24h. In the next day take the dough out the fridge and let it 2hrs at room temperature. After 2 hrs cut the dough in 4 pieces and start stretch it . Give it a round form ,add tomato sauce , mozzarella and whatever you want for topping. Add for 20mind at 250° and that is it . Enjoy your pizza.

Crush the biscuits in a food processor .add the melted butter and mix well. For the crust , put the biscuits in a springform pan and compact it. put in the fridge for aprox 30 mins. For the filling . soak the gelatin in the cold water, mix and let it soak,then put it in the hot milk and stir to dissolve. In the bowl of a mixer, put the cream cheese, powdered sugar and vanilla essence and mit well. Then add the whipping cream, increase the speed and mit the cream for 5 mins. Reduce the speed and add the mith and gelatin mixture, then increase the speed again Wash the fresh strawberries and cut them in halves. Arrange them all around the pan, then add the filling and spread it evenly. Put It in the fridge for 5-6 hrs. or overnight in a saucepan. For the jelly soak the gillatin for 5 mins. But the strawberis and add the sugar, then let it simmer for 5 mins. Smash the strawberries into a puree and let it coolfors min. then add the gelatin Pour it over the cake and put in the fridge for girot 2-3 hrs
